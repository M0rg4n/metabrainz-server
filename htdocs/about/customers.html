MetaBrainz Customers

The following companies have licensed our data for their own operations. This list does not include any individuals/companies that are using our Public Domain/Creative Commons data snapshots -- there are many users of this data beyond the licensees listed here:


 

The British Broadcasting Corporation (BBC) uses MusicBrainz data to provide detailed information about the artists who get played on their radio networks. The BBC also has a team of editors who are participating in editing the MusicBrainz data for use with their internal systems and the /music web pages.

 

Grooveshark is a peer-to-peer digital music brokerage and social community that rewards users for sharing their music. Grooveshark uses MusicBrainz data to fuel its music discovery system.

 

MusicIP is MusicBrainz' primary partner that licenses its music recommendation and identification technology. MusicBrainz uses the MusicIP identification service MusicDNS as part of its Picard Tagger. MusicIP also offers its Mixer application to end users to experience the power of MusicIP's automatic playlist generation tools.

 

Last.fm is a social music site that combines the power of social networking with the power of music to provide first class music recommendations. With Last.fm you can create your own music profile and listen to personalized radio stations that play your music recommendations.

 

Linkara Música -- Linkara is a Spanish social networking site (like MySpace, Tribe.net, etc). With Linkara Música users can rate their favorite music and then find others who share similar music tastes.

 

Thought Worthy Media -- Thought Worthy Media uses our Web Service to retrieve music information as part of their service offering that allows users to find, comment on and purchase popular media.

 

Spiral Vinyl Records -- Spiral Vinyl Records uses the MusicBrainz data to augment the data for their online vinyl record store.


Find out more about our data licenses and what data is included in MusicBrainz.
