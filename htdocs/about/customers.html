MetaBrainz Customers

The following companies have licensed our data for their own operations. This list does not include any individuals/companies that are using our Public Domain/Creative Commons data snapshots -- there are many users of this data beyond the licensees listed here:

Linkara Música -- Linkara is a Spanish social networking site (like MySpace, Trib.net, etc). With Linkara Música users can rate their favorite music and then find others who share similar music tastes.

Thought Worthy Media -- Thought Worthy Media uses our Web Service to retrieve music information as part of their service offering that allows users to find, comment on and purchase popular media.

Spiral Vinyl Records -- Spiral Vinyl Records uses the MusicBrainz data to augment the data for their online vinyl record store.
